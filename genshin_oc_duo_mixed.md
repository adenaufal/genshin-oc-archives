# Genshin Impact OC - Mixed Gender Duo

## Character 1: Dmitri Volkov

### Basic Info
- **Age**: 32 years old
- **Gender**: Male
- **Region**: Snezhnaya
- **Vision**: Dendro
- **Weapon**: Claymore
- **Constellation**: Silva Ferrum (Iron Forest)

### Physical Appearance
- **Height**: 180cm (tall and imposing)
- **Hair**: Platinum blonde, kept short and practical with slight wave
- **Eyes**: Crimson red, intense and sharp
- **Build**: Heavily muscular and scarred, built like a tank
- **Distinctive Features**: Multiple battle scars across his body, including a prominent one across his left cheek. Rough hands with calluses. Usually has a stern expression.

### Personality Snapshot
**Archetype**: Battle-hardened Warrior

Dmitri adalah veteran perang yang sudah mengalami countless battles sejak usia muda. Meski berasal dari Snezhnaya yang identik dengan Cryo, dia justru mendapat Dendro Vision - simbolis dari resilience dan growth-nya yang survive berbagai konflik. Personality-nya hardened tapi bukan cold; dia protective dan punya strong sense of duty.

### Personality Traits
- Stoic dan tidak banyak bicara, prefer action over words
- Disciplined dengan strong military background
- Protective terhadap yang lebih lemah, especially civilians
- Pragmatic dan straightforward - tidak suka politik atau manipulation
- Punya dry sense of humor yang jarang keluar
- Struggle dengan PTSD dari past battles, tapi trained untuk cope dengan healthy ways
- Surprisingly gentle dengan children dan animals

### Background (Brief)
Dmitri dulunya adalah elite soldier di Snezhnaya military, tapi keluar setelah witnessing corruption dan unnecessary violence dalam sebuah campaign 5 tahun lalu. Dendro Vision-nya datang bukan saat moment of violence, tapi justru ketika dia memutuskan untuk desert dan protect sekelompok refugees - manifestasi dari pilihan untuk nurture dan protect daripada destroy.

Sekarang dia bekerja sebagai mercenary/bodyguard freelance, carefully memilih jobs yang align dengan moral code-nya. Claymore-nya adalah warisan dari mentor yang killed in action.

### Combat Style
- **Playstyle**: Tanky DPS dengan crowd control dan sustain
- **Skill**: "Thorn Bulwark" - summon dendro barrier yang damage enemies yang attack dan heal Dmitri
- **Burst**: "Warden's Grove" - creates area of dendro spikes yang continuously damage dan slow enemies, buff Dmitri's defense

## Character 2: Lan Qingyu (兰青羽)

### Basic Info
- **Age**: 44 years old
- **Gender**: Female
- **Region**: Liyue
- **Vision**: Dendro
- **Weapon**: Sword
- **Constellation**: Serpens Nebula (Mist Serpent)

### Physical Appearance
- **Height**: 165cm (average height)
- **Hair**: Platinum blonde (unusual for Liyue native), elegant updo dengan jade hairpins
- **Eyes**: Teal, calculating dan expressive when she wants them to be
- **Build**: Slender dan graceful, moves like a dancer
- **Distinctive Features**: Elegant makeup, beauty mark under her left eye, always impeccably dressed. Hands are deceptively delicate-looking tapi trained. Smile yang sophisticated tapi slightly enigmatic.

### Personality Snapshot
**Archetype**: Cunning Spy

Lan Qingyu adalah master of deception dan information broker yang operate dari shadows of Liyue Harbor. Di age 44, dia sudah decades of experience dalam espionage, manipulation, dan gathering secrets. Dia punya dual personas - elegant socialite di public, cold professional di shadows.

### Personality Traits
- Highly intelligent dengan exceptional memory dan analytical skills
- Charming dan sophisticated dalam social situations - master of reading people
- Pragmatic dan morally gray - loyalty goes to highest bidder (mostly)
- Patient dan strategic, prefer long-term plans over impulsive actions
- Surprisingly sentimental tentang certain things dari past-nya
- Protective terhadap her network of informants, treat them well
- Enjoy fine things in life - good tea, silk clothes, refined arts
- Can be ruthless when necessary, tapi prefer elegant solutions over violence

### Background (Brief)
Qingyu lahir di lower-class family di Liyue, tapi sejak muda dia punya talent untuk manipulation dan reading people. Dia climb social ladder melalui strategic connections dan blackmail, eventually becoming one of the most connected information brokers di Liyue Harbor.

Dendro Vision-nya came di age 30, setelah bertahun-tahun building her network - manifestasi dari kemampuannya untuk "grow" connections dan nurture web of information. Her platinum blonde hair adalah unusual genetic trait yang actually help her stand out dan be memorable in the right ways.

Sekarang di 44, dia punya established position, vast network, dan enough wealth untuk comfortable life. Tapi dia masih active karena she genuinely enjoys the game of secrets dan information.

### Combat Style
- **Playstyle**: Agile Sub-DPS/Support dengan DoT dan debuffs
- **Skill**: "Poisoned Silk" - quick dash yang leave dendro trail, apply poison DoT
- **Burst**: "Web of Thorns" - create network of dendro threads yang immobilize enemies dan continuously drain HP

## Dynamic Duo Concept

Dmitri dan Qingyu adalah interesting contrast sebagai duo:

- **Age gap**: 12 tahun difference, both matured adults tapi different life stages
- **Moral spectrum**: Dmitri's clear moral code vs Qingyu's moral flexibility
- **Combat style**: Direct brutal force (Dmitri) vs elegant subtle tactics (Qingyu)
- **Personality**: Straightforward warrior vs manipulative spy
- **Same Vision**: Both Dendro - interesting karena represent different aspects (resilience/growth vs connections/networks)
- **Gender dynamic**: Mature professional partnership tanpa romance clichés

### Potential Story Hook
- **Employer-Employee**: Qingyu hire Dmitri sebagai muscle for dangerous job yang require both brain dan brawn
- **Uneasy Alliance**: Forced to work together, initially distrust each other (Dmitri distrust spies, Qingyu thinks warriors are simple-minded)
- **Mutual Respect**: Eventually develop professional respect - Dmitri appreciate Qingyu's strategic mind, Qingyu appreciate Dmitri's unwavering principles
- **Contrast Philosophy**: Interesting dialogues tentang morality, duty, survival dalam morally gray world

## Art Direction Notes

### Dmitri Volkov
- **Color palette**: Cold neutrals - platinum blonde, crimson eyes, Snezhnaya whites/grays, dendro green accents
- **Outfit**: Modified military uniform dari Snezhnaya - practical armor pieces, fur-trimmed coat (removed insignias), combat boots. Battle-worn but maintained.
- **Weapon**: Large intimidating claymore dengan dendro crystals embedded dalam blade
- **Pose ideas**: Strong defensive stance, protective posture, atau mid-swing dengan claymore
- **Expression**: Stern, focused, slightly tired eyes yang show experience
- **Design notes**: Scars are important - show history. Contrast antara brutal weapon dengan dendro (life) vision

### Lan Qingyu
- **Color palette**: Elegant Liyue colors - platinum blonde (unusual/striking), teal eyes, jade green, gold, deep burgundy
- **Outfit**: Elegant modified qipao atau hanfu dengan modern spy elements - concealed weapon sheaths, practical yet beautiful. Silk materials, jade accessories
- **Weapon**: Elegant sword yang looks decorative tapi deadly sharp
- **Pose ideas**: Graceful sword stance, atau social pose dengan fan/teacup tapi hint of danger
- **Expression**: Sophisticated smile, calculating eyes, atau cold professional look
- **Design notes**: Emphasize elegance dan deception - beautiful exterior hiding dangerous interior

### Composition Ideas
- **Contrast**: Brute force vs elegant deception, direct vs indirect, light armor vs formal clothes
- **Same Vision**: Both use dendro tapi different manifestations - his is protective/defensive growth, hers is entrapping/controlling vines
- **Age difference**: Show maturity dalam both - his through scars/weariness, hers through elegance/poise
- **Professional partnership**: Pose them together tapi not romantically - more like business partners
- **Background split**: Snezhnaya snow/battlefield (Dmitri) vs Liyue Harbor night/teahouse (Qingyu)
